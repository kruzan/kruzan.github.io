Adobo


Filipino Chicken Adobo is a standard in any Filipino household. Adobo refers to a cooking method indigenous to the Philippines. Vinegar is one of the key ingredients in Adobo and in much of Filipino cuisine (and culture!). Vinegar was used often when cooking or preparing food to keep them fresh longer in the tropic climate of the Philippines.


Ingredients


  • 4 tsp of sesame oil (optional)
  • 1 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp of peppercorns
  • 1 kg chicken thighs and legs ### Directions 1. Put all ingredients (except for chicken) in a bowl. 2. Mix well. You can taste the sauce. It should taste very tangy. 3. Add chicken to bowl and make sure chicken is completely coated. 4. Marinate for at least 30 min. 5. Cook over the medium heat for around 1 hour. 6. Serve with white rice.
