Ciasto Drożdżowe

This recipe is from Babcia Sabina and one of our favourites cakes. Rosemarie valiantly tried to make it for over 2 decades. It was only after moving to Ireland did her attempts come close. It is a great cake for anytime of day especially with a cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients


  • ½ cup raisins
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 ½ cup sugar
  • 3 to 4 bags of fast action yeast
  • 1 ½ cup milk (divided)
  • 2 lbs of flour
  • 2 ½ sticks of butter or margarine (290 g)
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Directions


  1. In a bowl, mix the yeast with a 1 TBSP sugar and 1 TBSP of flour. Add ½ cup of milk. The milk should be warm but not boiling. Mix well. Cover the bowl with a tea towel and put aside to rise. Let it rise for around 30 minutes. Afterwards, the mixture should look very bubbly.
  2. While the yeast is rising, soak the raisins in hot water
  3. Combine the egg yolks and sugar in a small bowl. Mix well
  4. In a large mixing bowl or stand mixer, add the flour. Slowly add the yeast. Mix the mixture well.
  5. Slowly add the eggs and 1 cup of mix. (alternating each one).
  6. Slowly add butter/margarine and vanilla..
  7. Knead well. The mixture will be sticky and wet.
  8. Cover the bowl with a tea towel and let it rise in a warm area.
  9. After an hour, it should double in size. Knead again and add the raisins.
  10. Grease and flour a large pan (approx. 12 x 9 in)
  11. Add dough to pan and cover with tea towel. Let it rise again for one hour.
  12. Bake at 350 F for 45 minutes
