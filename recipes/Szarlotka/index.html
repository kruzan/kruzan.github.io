Szarlotka

This recipe is from Babcia Sabina and is Wieniek’s favorite dessert. It is a cake with apple filling with a crumble topping. Wieniek likes it on its own but Rosemarie prefers it with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

Cake


  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 TBL sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup of butter, room temperature – should be soft but not melted

Filling


  • 6 Granny Smith or baking apples
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Directions


  1. Put the flour in a pile and mix all dry ingredients
  2. Mix in egg yolks
  3. Cut in the butter (with two knives, or rub into flour with fingers) until it resembles coarse meal
  4. Knead the cake mix for a few minutes. The mixture will be crumbly.
  5. Form a big ball and wrap with plastic wrap.
  6. Keep in the refrigerator for at least 30 min.
  7. Divide the dough into two.
  8. Grease baking pan (13 x 9 inches)
  9. Evenly press ½ dough onto bottom of pan.
  10. Peel and grate apples.
  11. Place apple on top of dough in plan.
  12. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar over apples.
  13. Sprinkle remaining dough over apples.
  14. Bake at 350 F for 45 minutes
