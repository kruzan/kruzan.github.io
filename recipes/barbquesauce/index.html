Barbque Sauce


A basic barbque sauce great for chicken. When using on chicken, brush sauce on checking during the last 10 to 15 minutes of cooking.

  • Prep time: 5 min
  • Cook time: 20 min

Ingredients


  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 ½ cups ketchup
  • ½ cup cider vinegar
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Directions


  1. Heat oil in saucepan.
  2. Add onions and garlic. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, sugar, chili powder and cayenne.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer partially covered for about 20 minutes.
