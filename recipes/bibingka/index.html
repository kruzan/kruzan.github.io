Bibingka


Bibingka is a type of baked rice cake from. No celebration in the Philippines would be complete without Bibingka.


Ingredients


  • 400 grams glutinous rice flour or sweet rice flour (found in any Asian grocery store)
  • 165 grams butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 4 eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 can of condensed milk (for topping)

Directions


  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Melt the butter in the large baking dish and remove.
  3. Mix all dry ingredients together in a large bowl
  4. Slowly add the milk to the mixture
  5. Then slowly add the coco cream
  6. Add the eggs (slightly beaten)
  7. Now add the butter and mix. Before removing butter from dish, make sure sides of baking dish is well buttered.
  8. Pour the mixture into the baking dish
  9. Bake for 40 mins in preheated oven
  10. Remove from oven. The cake will have risen in areas. Flatten surface with a fork so it is level.
  11. Pour the condensed milk onto it and spread it out.
