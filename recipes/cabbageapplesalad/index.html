Cabbage and Apples

This is a great summertime salad. Perfect for a barbque.

Ingredients


  • 1 small head of cabbage (thinly sliced) - white or Peking cabbage
  • 3 tablespoon leek (chopped)
  • 3 to 4 apples grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon dill
  • 2 tablespoon parsley

Directions


  1. Mix all ingredients in large bowl.
  2. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
  3. Enjoy
