Chicken Wings


Grandma’s recipe for chicken wings


INGREDIENTS


  • 5 lb chicken wings
  • 1 1/2 pound ???
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ???
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped ginger

DIRECTIONS


  1. Set oven to 375ºF
  2. Season chicken wings
  3. Bake for 45mins in preheated oven
  4. Remove and mix with Hassan(????) sauce
  5. To make Hassan sauce, mix together the soy sauce, honey, sugar, ginger, garlic (and the stuff on the list that cant read)
  6. Put back in the oven for 5 more minutes at 250ºF
