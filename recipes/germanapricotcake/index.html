Apricot Cake

When Rose was living in Poland, she house sat and cat sat for an American couple when they went on vacation. The couple had an apricot tree which was great in this recipe. The wife gave this recipe to Rose. It is a delicious summertime dessert.


  • Prep time: 20 min
  • Cook time: 50 min

Ingredients


  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ½ cup sugar
  • 3 eggs beaten
  • 1 cup butter
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • canned or fresh apricots, quartered

Directions


  1. Cream butter and sugar together
  2. Add beaten eggs.
  3. Mix in flour, salt and vanilla
  4. Pour batter in greased 9 x 12 pan, reserving one cup of batter.
  5. Arrange the fresh apricots or filling on top of the batter.
  6. Spread the remaining batter on top of filling.
  7. Bake for 50 minutes at 350 F.
