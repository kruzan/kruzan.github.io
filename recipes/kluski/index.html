Kluski Kładzione

A Polish dish, usually eaten with beef goulash (see previous page)

INGREDIENTS


  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 125grams heavy cream
  • 3-4 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg

DIRECTIONS


  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. Put flour, eggs, cream, salt, pepper and nutmeg into a large bowl
  3. Mix until you get a smooth paste
  4. Spread the dough onto a chopping board and cut with a knife
  5. Throw the pieces into the boiling water
  6. Once boiled, remove with a slotted spoon.
