Lamb Tangine


Serves four moderately hungry people


INGREDIENTS


  • 2 pounds lamb
  • 1 tablespoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 large diced onions
  • 3 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 4 ounces/ 100grams dried apricots (chopped)
  • 2 ounces/ 50grams raisins
  • 1/2 pint/ 500ml tomato juice
  • 1/4 pint/ 100ml beef/lamb stock
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • juice of 1/2 a lemon

DIRECTIONS


  1. Preheat oven to 180ÂºC (gas mark 4)
  2. Mix spices together and coat meat with the spices
  3. Heat half the oil and onions in a large pan
  4. Once onions are brown place in a flameproof casserole
  5. Now brown meat in the remaining oil (TIP: brown meat in batches to get the meat a good color)
  6. Transfer meat to the casserole and add the remaining ingredients
  7. Stir well and bring to a boil
  8. Place in oven for approximately 1 1/2 hours or until meat is very tender
