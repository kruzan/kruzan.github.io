Lumpia


There are many lumpia recipes. This one is the one that mom gave me when I was away in college and I have been using it since.

  • Prep time: 40 min
  • Cook time: 40 min
  • Serving: 30 long lumpias or 60 mini

Ingredients


  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 large potatoes, chopped into small cubes
  • 2 carrots, julienne-cut
  • 1 med onion, diced
  • 2 garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon rice floor (optional)
  • 3 egg
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • lumpia wrappers or spring roll wrappers

Directions


  1. Beat one of the eggs in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Add the rest of ingredients to a large box and mix gently thoroughly using your hands or a silicone spatula
  3. Place three tablespoons of the filling across a wrapper. Roll the wrapper over the filling. Keep the roll as tight as possible.
  4. Moisten the other side of the wrapper with egg to seal the edge.
  5. Heat a heavy skillet (frying pan) over medium heat, add oil to 1 1/2 cm depth, and heat for 5 minutes.
  6. Fry the rolls for 7 to 10 minutes. Gently turning the lumpia until all sides are golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Enjoy! It is great with sweet chili sauce.
