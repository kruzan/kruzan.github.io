Pancit Bihon


  • Prep time: 45 min
  • Cook time: 60 min

Ingredients:


  • 1 ½ lbs. chicken with bones and skin on
  • 3 chinese style sausage (very hard to find in Ireland) - optional
  • 1 package bihon noodles
  • 2 garlic minced
  • 1 small onion finely chopped
  • ½ cabbage thinly sliced
  • 3 carrots julienne cut
  • 2 celery stalks, cut cross section
  • 10 string beans, cut in half
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 green onions, white and light section only, diced
  • 1 lemon/calamansi, cut into wedges

Directions:


  1. In medium sized pot, boil chicken with bones and sausages in 2 cups slightly salted water for 30 minutes. Remove chicken and sausages to cool before handling. Save the broth!!
  2. Once cool, remove the chicken from bone and shred. Slice sausages into thin slices. Throw out bones
  3. While chicken is cooling, soak noodles in warm water in large bowl. Set aside.
  4. Heat 2 tbsp oil in large pan or work.
  5. Stir fry cabbage and carrots for a few minutes. Remove from pan and set aside.
  6. Heat remaining oil.
  7. Saute chopped garlic and onions. Cook for a few minutes.
  8. Add sausages and chicken.
  9. Add 3 tablespoons soy sauce. Stir constantly.
  10. Add celery and beans and broth from chicken/sausage pot.
  11. Bring to a boil.
  12. Add noodles to main mixture and simmer on low flame.
  13. Add cabbage and carrots to pan.
  14. Continue to stir to break up noodles.
  15. Add soy sauce and black pepper to taste.
  16. Stir until noodles are soft and flavored (approx. 5-10 more minutes).

Serve hot. Garnish with green onion (sliced). You can also put boiled eggs (cut in half) on top. Season with lemon/calamansi wedge juice.
