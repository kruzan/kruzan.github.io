Bean Soup


A Polish dish, made often by our Babcia (Polish grandmother)


INGREDIENTS


  • 2 liters of water (can be stocked cube)
  • 1.5 cups of white beans
  • 200grams boneless beef
  • 4 small potatoes
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 onion
  • clove of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • paprika
  • marjoram
  • salt
  • pepper

DIRECTIONS


  1. Rinse the beans, pour the water into a pot, boil and leave for 2-4 hours
  2. Wash meat, cut into cubes, fry in oil and add the onion (chopped)
  3. When the water evaporates, add the flour and mix lightly
  4. Add the beans and cook (about 30mins)
  5. Add the diced potatoes and cook for about 15mins
  6. Add the tomato paste, chopped garlic and spices
  7. Season to taste
