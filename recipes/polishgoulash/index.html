Beef Goulash

A Polish dish, usually eaten with “kluski” (see next page)


  • Prep time: 30 min
  • Cooking time: 45 min
  • Serves: 6

Ingredients


  • 750 g beef, sliced
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3-4 dried mushrooms
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ tsp Maggi sauce (this contain MSG so use sparingly)
  • 250 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions


  1. Soak dry mushrooms in a small bowl of water. Set aside.
  2. Season with beef with pepper.
  3. Dredge beef in flour. Shake off excess.
  4. Heat oil in Dutch oven or large pot.
  5. Cook beef in batches for a few minutes and remove from pan.
  6. Add onion and garlic to pot. Cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Return beef to pot.
  8. Add soaking mushrooms, bay leaf, and Maggi sauce.
  9. Add 1 cup of water.
  10. While meat is cooking, sauté fresh mushrooms in butter and garlic for 5 minutes.
  11. Add mushrooms to beef and continue cooking.
  12. At the end, season with salt and pepper.
  13. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
