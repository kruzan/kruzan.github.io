Saucepan Cookies


This recipe comes from the Hylton family (Patsy, Shelley and Lori). We loved these blondie type brownies growing up and we beg them to make them all the time.

  • Prep: 20 min
  • Cook time: 35 min

Ingredients


  • 2/3 cup butter or margarine (150 grams)
  • 1 lb box brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 ¾ cup flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 bag chocolate chips

    Directions


    1. Melt butter in large saucepan
    2. Turn off heat.
    3. Stir in brown sugar.
    4. Stir in eggs.
    5. Gradually add flour, salt and backing poweder.
    6. Add chocolate chips
    7. Pour batter in 9 x 12 inch baking pan lined with foil.
    8. Bake at 350 F for 30 to 35 minutes.
    9. Cookies are done when toothpick comes out cleanly.
