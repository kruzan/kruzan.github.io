Feta-Spinach Salad


  • Prep time: 25 min
  • Cook time: 0 min
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients


  • ¾ lb fresh spinach (washed, trimmed and torn) – ½ lb if already washed & ready for use
  • 2 med tart green apples, cored and sliced
  • 3 tbsp rice wine vinegar, divided
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • 1 ½ tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ¾ cup (3 oz) feta cheese, crumbled

Directions


  1. Toss applies with 1 tbsp vinegar, set aside
  2. Place spinach in large salad bowl.
  3. Process remaining vinegar with olive oil, juice, honey and mustard in blender then pour spinach and toss well.
  4. Sprinkle with apples and cheese.
