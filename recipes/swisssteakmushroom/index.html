Swiss Steak


A favourite meal for any occasion. The sauce really makes this a special meal. Serve with white rice.

  • Prep time: 15 min
  • Cook time: 40 min
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients


  • ½ lb mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ stick butter
  • 3 lb sirloin or swiss steak, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Soy sauce, 3 tbsp, divided
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • ½ tsp lime juice

Directions


  1. In a small bowl, mix 1 tbsp soy sauce and flour together. It should have a paste consistency.
  2. Add lime juice.
  3. Slowly add ½ cup of water.
  4. Set bowl aside.
  5. In frying pan, heat 1 tsp oil.
  6. Add 1 garlic and butter.
  7. Add mushrooms.
  8. Cook until mushrooms are brown.
  9. Remove mushrooms to plate.
  10. Add remaining oil to pan.
  11. Add onion and garlic. Cook for a few minutes.
  12. Add sliced beef.
  13. When the beef is no longer red, add ¼ cup water and simmer for 20 minutes.
  14. If tender, add black pepper and soy sauce to taste.
  15. Slowly add sauce from bowl. Cook for 3 minutes. The sauce should thicken.
  16. Add mushrooms and cook for another 3 minutes.
  17. Test and adjust seasoning.
  18. Serve hot with white rice.
